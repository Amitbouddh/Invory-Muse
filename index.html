The Heart Behind
Ivory Muse

Hi, I'm Priya — the sole maker behind every Ivory Muse creation. Based in Pune, I handcraft each piece from premium New Zealand wool thread in my sunlit studio.

What started as a pandemic hobby in 2020 quickly became a passion, then a purpose. Every petal I sculpt carries the intention of bringing lasting joy to someone's home or heart.

Ivory Muse was born from a childhood memory — my grandmother's hands weaving colorful threads into flowers that lasted decades. I wanted to carry that love forward.
